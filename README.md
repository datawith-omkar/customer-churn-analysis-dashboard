# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior to identify patterns, understand customer retention, and provide business insights. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor churn rates and identify high-risk customer segments.

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX

---

## Business Objective

- Monitor customer churn rate
- Identify factors affecting customer retention
- Analyze churn by customer segments
- Support data-driven retention strategies

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains customer-related information including:

- Customer ID
- Gender
- Senior Citizen
- Partner
- Dependents
- Contract Type
- Payment Method
- Tenure
- Monthly Charges
- Total Charges
- Churn Status

---

## Dashboard KPIs

- Total Customers
- Active Customers
- Churned Customers
- Churn Rate (%)

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1: Executive Summary
- Total Customers
- Churn Rate
- Active vs Churned Customers
- Customer Overview

### Page 2: Customer Segmentation
- Churn by Gender
- Churn by Contract Type
- Churn by Payment Method
- Churn by Senior Citizen

### Page 3: Retention Analysis
- Tenure Distribution
- Monthly Charges Analysis
- High-Risk Customer Segments

---

## Key Insights

- Month-to-Month contract customers have the highest churn rate.
- Customers using Electronic Check are more likely to churn.
- Customers with shorter tenure show higher churn probability.
- Long-term customers demonstrate better retention.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Encourage long-term contracts through loyalty programs.
- Improve onboarding experience for new customers.
- Develop retention campaigns for high-risk segments.
- Review customer experience for electronic payment users.
